Nose-feeted Foot-nosed Elephant

My elephant is very grumpy, he has four noses and one foot, he is a freak among other elephants, because he uses his noses to walk and his foot to sniff around.

Everybody is freaked out by this giant guy, except me. This four-nosed creature is just like a huge octopus wriggling on land, which is kind of lovely.

  • Nose-feeted: nose to walk
  • Foot-nosed: foot to sniff
